Boba Drops for Clubs 🧋

Boba Drops is a Hack Club program where your members build personal websites and get rewarded with a grant to buy boba. Run it as a workshop at your next meeting, no experience needed, and it works for complete beginners. Clubs are welcome from anywhere in the world!

Running a workshop

Host a Boba Drops workshop at your club meeting. Members will build their own personal website using HTML & CSS, host it on GitHub Pages, and submit it. Each approved submission earns them $5 for boba. You can use our workshop guide to run the session, it walks through everything step by step.

More of a social coder? Your members can also hang out online with other high schoolers building their first websites.

Before you run it...

Check out the requirements & examples page so you know what makes a passing submission, and so you can set expectations with your club members before they start building.
