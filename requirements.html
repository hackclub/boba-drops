Requirements & Examples

Everything your club members need to know before submitting, plus three real examples showing exactly what we're looking for.

The Requirements

  • Ages 13–18 only. Other folks can join club meetings, but only participants in this age range can ship a project to Boba Drops.
  • No AI. Websites must be hand-written. Workshops with too many AI submissions risk being closed, and in some cases, so does the club. Set expectations early.
  • Websites must be unique. Each participant builds their own site. We'll reject submissions that all use the same HTML & CSS features. Encourage members to experiment with different tags!
  • HTML & CSS only. No JavaScript or other languages, this program is for beginners mastering the basics. There are other Hack Club programs for JS!
  • At least 5 HTML & CSS features. With at least one being CSS and at least one being an image.
  • Good effort & accessible. No tiny fonts, excessive color clashes, or illegible text. It doesn't have to be beautiful, it just has to be readable and genuine.
